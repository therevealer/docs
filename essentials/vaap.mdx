## VAAP — Value Alignment & Participation Principles

The **Value Alignment & Participation Principles (VAAP)** define how contributions, participation, and reward mechanisms are aligned with zkPass DAO’s mission of building a secure, privacy-preserving data proof protocol.

### Why VAAP Matters

Traditional DAOs often struggle with:

- token concentration and power centralization
- sybil influence without meaningful contribution evidence
- shallow engagement without long-term commitment

VAAP ensures that participation is meaningful, merit-based, and reflective of verified contributions rather than only capital ownership.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. **Proof-First Participation**

Decentralized participation is built on the strength of **verifiable contributions** backed by zkPass proofs and reputation signals.

Proofs demonstrate:
- repeated activity
- real value delivered to the ecosystem
- sustained engagement over time

This approach mitigates sybil attacks and increases trust in governance decisions.

---

### 2. **Contribution-Weighted Influence**

Beyond token balance, influence is shaped by the **quality and depth** of contributions:

- protocol development
- technical reviews
- educational content creation
- ecosystem integrations
- community stewardship

These actions are quantified through zkPass proofs and reward structures.

---

### 3. **Sybil Resistance & Credibility**

VAAP enhances sybil resistance by pairing:

- cryptographic proofs
- identity verification signals
- participation history

This makes governance resistant to attack while honoring privacy.

---

### 4. **Alignment with Community Goals**

Governance decisions are aligned to long-term outcomes such as:

- protocol security
- ecosystem growth
- reputation preservation
- economic sustainability

Selections of proposals, stewards, or contributors are weighted by how well they advance these goals.

---

### 5. **Open & Transparent Coordination**

VAAP embraces transparency:

- all governance actions are public
- rationale for decisions is documented
- community feedback is encouraged

This builds trust, not just governance mechanics.

---

## How VAAP Works in Practice

VAAP informs:

- reputation-informed voting models
- eligibility for DAO roles
- participation incentives and grants
- curator recommendations and review bodies

As with governance thresholds and processes, VAAP may evolve through DAO-approved refinements.

---

## Summary

VAAP ensures that zkPass DAO:

- values **real contribution over raw capital**
- resists manipulation through cryptographic and reputation-based resistance
- builds a governance model with **trustworthy participation**
- aligns community incentives with long-term protocol health

## $ZKP Token

The **$ZKP Token** is a coordination and governance token at the heart of the zkPass ecosystem.

It empowers:

- governance participation
- ecosystem alignment incentives
- verifiable contribution weighting
- decentralized coordination

> The $ZKP token is **not equity**, **not investment property**, and **not a claim on DAO assets or profits**.

---

## Utility of $ZKP

### Governance Participation

$ZKP is the primary mechanism for:

- voting on proposals
- signaling community priorities
- shaping treasury allocation
- establishing protocol parameters

Each token provides a baseline vote, with optional reputation-enhanced mechanisms where explicitly defined.

---

## Role in Reputation-Informed Governance

zkPass introduces optional reputation layers such as:

- weighted voting for proofs and contributions
- reputation signals in special governance categories
- proof-gated participation for select proposal types

These mechanisms are opt-in and do not replace established quorum rules or token dominance safeguards.

## Compliance & Positioning

From a regulatory compliance perspective:

- zkPass DAO is a **non-profit Swiss Association**
- $ZKP is a **governance/coordination tool**
- it is **not profit-bearing**
- it does not confer equity or fiduciary claims

This positioning supports:
- compliant token listings
- clear legal separation from investment instruments
- protocol safety as a coordination primitive

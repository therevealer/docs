## 2025 Roadmap

---

## Q1 2025 — Core Protocol & Developer Foundations

- **zkTLS Protocol Upgrade**  
  Faster prover speed, reduced memory usage, and mobile-optimized proof generation.

- **zkTLS Audits**  
  Completion of formal verification and multi-firm zero-knowledge security audits.

- **zkPass SDK v2.0**  
  Unified SDK supporting web, mobile, and browser extensions.

- **Proof UX Toolkit**  
  Prebuilt modules for verifiable login, proof-of-access, and off-chain reputation.

- **DevHub v2**  
  Enhanced developer hub with schema explorer, real-time analytics, and customizable proof flows.

---

## Q2 2025 — Proof Applications & Ecosystem Expansion

- **Verifiable Data Portal**  
  Community-curated schema marketplace for Web2 data integrations.

- **Consumer Applications Launch**  
  zk-powered tools including Web2 airdrop verification, DeFi credit scoring, gated airdrops, and sybil-resistant voting.

- **zkPass Node Network Expansion**  
  Browser-based and mobile node clients to scale decentralized proof validation.

- **Verifiable Economy API**  
  SDK modules for tokenizing credentials such as Duolingo streaks, GitHub contributions, and ride history.

---

## Q3 2025 — Governance & Institutional Pilots

- **Zero-Knowledge Compliance Suite**  
  KYC and KYB solutions enabling identity verification without exposing private data.

- **Enterprise & Institutional Pilots**  
  MVP launch of the zkPass Institutional Suite with banks, education, and healthcare providers.

- **Country Partner Initiatives**  
  Collaborations with national ecosystems for zk-verifiable credentials (e.g. tax records, university status, proof of employment).

- **zkPass Reputation Framework**  
  Reputation scoring combining social, financial, and educational proofs.

---

## Q4 2025 — Token Launch & Network Maturity

- **Token Generation Event (TGE)**  
  Launch of the $ZKP token powering staking, incentives, governance, and reputation rewards.

- **Token Utility Framework**  
  Activate $ZKP across node staking, proof verification, schema access, and reward distribution.

- **Governance Expansion**  
  Broaden DAO participation, treasury allocation, and incentive mechanisms.

- **Network Scaling**  
  Increased throughput and validator coverage for consumer and enterprise-grade adoption.
